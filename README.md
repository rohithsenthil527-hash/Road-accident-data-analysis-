Problem Statement – Road Accident Data Analysis
Road accidents are a major public safety concern, resulting in loss of lives, injuries, and property damage. Large volumes of accident data are generated by traffic departments and government agencies, but this data is often underutilized. The challenge is to analyze road accident data to identify accident-prone locations, contributing factors, time-based patterns, and vehicle-related causes. By applying data analysis techniques, meaningful insights can be obtained to support decision-making and improve road safety measures.
Objectives
Analyze accident data to identify trends and patterns in road accidents.
Identify accident hotspots where accidents occur most frequently.
Study the impact of factors such as weather, road conditions, vehicle type, and driver behavior on accidents.
Examine temporal patterns based on time, day, month, and season.
Visualize accident statistics using charts, graphs, and dashboards.
Predict accident severity or occurrence using data analysis and machine learning techniques.
Provide recommendations to traffic authorities for improving road safety and reducing accidents.
Expected Outcome
Identification of high-risk accident zones.
Better understanding of major accident causes.
Data-driven recommendations for accident prevention.
Interactive dashboard for monitoring and analyzing accident trends.
